The Problem We Solve

In an era where 40% of online reviews are estimated to be fake or incentivized, consumers are losing trust in star ratings.

Our mission is simple: to restore that trust. We provide a transparent, algorithmic "Trust Score" for every product, helping you distinguish between genuine buyer feedback and paid promotion.

Our Technology

Our proprietary AI engine doesn't just look at the average rating. It analyzes the linguistic structure and metadata of the latest 100 reviews in real-time.

  • Pattern Detection:

    Identifying repetitive phrases and "template-style" reviews used by paid review farms.

  • Account Verification:

    Filtering out low-authority or "one-day" accounts that flood products with unearned praise.

  • Privacy First:

    We don't track your identity. Our system uses cookies only to facilitate safe redirects to official stores.
