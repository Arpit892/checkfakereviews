About Us

Welcome to CheckFakeReviews, India's first dedicated AI-powered tool designed to detect inauthentic product reviews on major e-commerce platforms.

Our Mission

In an era where 40% of online reviews are estimated to be fake or incentivized, consumers are losing trust in star ratings. Our mission is to restore that trust by providing a transparent, algorithmic "Trust Score" for every product.

How It Works

We do not just count stars. Our Python-based engine scrapes the latest 100 reviews and analyzes them for:

  • Linguistic Repetition: Detecting "copy-paste" bot patterns.
  • Data Density: Flagging low-effort, one-word reviews.
  • Sentiment Flooding: Identifying excessive punctuation and unnatural praise.
