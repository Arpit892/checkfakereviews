Is This Product Safe? Check Amazon & Flipkart Reviews Instantly

Shopping online is risky. Sellers on Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra often buy fake 5-star reviews to trick you into buying low-quality products. CheckFakeReviews.online is a free AI-powered tool that analyzes review patterns to detect scams.

How to Spot Fake Reviews?

Our algorithm looks for these 3 warning signs:

  • Repetitive Phrasing: Bots often use the exact same sentences across hundreds of reviews.
  • Review Bursting: A sudden spike of 5-star reviews in a single day is a major red flag.
  • Generic Profiles: Reviews from users with no profile pictures often indicate bot farms.
